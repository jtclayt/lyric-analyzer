Lyric Uniqueness Analyzer

About this site

This website uses the lyrics.ovh API to do some simple analysis on the unique words that an artist uses in their songs. The idea for this comes from a vocabulary analysis of Hip Hop artists done by Matt Daniels. Link

To use this simply enter an artist and a song to add the words in the lyrics to the data set. Simple token analysis will be done on the words in the lyrics to determine the percentage of unique words per artist. As more songs are added from each artist the percentage will represent an aggregate of all the songs queried for. The data will then be loaded on a D3 bar chart which will graphically represent the percent of unique words in all the songs that have been requested. Cards for each artist will be added below the chart with more detailed information.

A note on uniqueness: The tokens would consider words like don't and do not different. Also, slang and purposely misspelled words will be tokenized as unique.
